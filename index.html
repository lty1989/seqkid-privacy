Privacy Policy for SeqKid

Effective Date: July 17, 2026  |  Last Updated: July 17, 2026

Developer: liutianyi App: SeqKid — Visual Attention Training Platform: Android Ages: 6+
✅ SeqKid collects no personal data, connects to no servers, and shares nothing with anyone — ever.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

SeqKid does not collect, transmit, store on any server, or share any of the following:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email address, phone number, user ID)
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, Android ID, advertising ID)
  • Location data (GPS, network-based, or inferred)
  • Contact lists, call logs, or messages
  • Photos, videos, or files from device storage
  • Microphone or camera input
  • Browsing history or app usage analytics
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data sent off-device
  • Payment or financial information

2. Internet Access

SeqKid does not declare the INTERNET permission in its AndroidManifest.xml. The app is technically incapable of making any network connection. There is no analytics endpoint, no ad network, no remote configuration, and no telemetry of any kind.

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

SeqKid stores a small amount of game data locally on your device only, in the app's private internal storage directory (filesDir). This data is never accessible to other apps and is automatically deleted when you uninstall SeqKid.

DataPurpose
Game session records (time, mistakes, star rating, difficulty)Display your personal history and best scores
Best scores per difficulty levelShow your personal best on the results screen
App settings (sound, dark mode, reminders)Remember your preferences between sessions

This data exists solely on your device and never leaves it.

You can delete all stored data at any time via Settings → Clear Training Data inside the app.

4. Permissions Requested

SeqKid requests only one Android permission:

PermissionReason
WAKE_LOCKPrevents the screen from turning off while a game session is in progress

No storage permission, no camera permission, no microphone permission, no location permission, no contacts permission, and no network permission are requested or used.

5. Third-Party SDKs and Services

SeqKid contains no third-party SDKs of any kind. Specifically, it does not include:

  • Advertising SDKs (e.g., Google AdMob, Meta Audience Network)
  • Analytics SDKs (e.g., Firebase Analytics, Mixpanel, Amplitude)
  • Crash-reporting SDKs (e.g., Crashlytics, Sentry)
  • Social login SDKs (e.g., Google Sign-In, Facebook Login)
  • Push notification services
  • Any other service that communicates with external servers

All libraries used (Flutter framework, Riverpod, Isar, GoRouter, etc.) operate entirely on-device and do not transmit data externally.

6. Children's Privacy

SeqKid is designed for children aged 6 and above. Because the app collects no personal information whatsoever — from users of any age — it is compliant with children's privacy requirements including COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and GDPR-K provisions.

We do not knowingly collect any information from children or adults. There is nothing to collect.

7. Data Security

Because SeqKid does not transmit any data over a network and stores all data locally in the app's private sandbox, there is no external data transmission to secure. Local data is protected by Android's standard app sandboxing — no other app can read SeqKid's data without root access.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

All local game data is retained until you either:

  • Delete it manually via Settings → Clear Training Data, or
  • Uninstall the app (Android automatically removes all app-private data on uninstall)

There is no data on any server to delete, as none is ever sent there.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

If a future update of SeqKid were to change how data is handled, this Privacy Policy would be updated before that version is published, and the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document would be revised. Continued use of the updated version constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

We do not anticipate any changes that would introduce data collection, as offline-first and privacy-by-design are core principles of this app.
